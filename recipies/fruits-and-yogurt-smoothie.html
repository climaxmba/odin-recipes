Fruits-and Yogurt Smoothie

Description

This yogurt smoothie recipe is delicious! You may substitute the strawberries for any other berries or fruit.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • ¼ cup pineapple juice
  • 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

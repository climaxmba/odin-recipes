Mac and cheese

Description

To make this rich mac and cheese recipe healthier we use whole wheat bread crumbs, low-fat milk and cheese, whole wheat noodles, and reduced the amount of butter. Serve with a salad for a healthy meatless dinner.

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Green beans
  • Spring onions
  • Lettuce
  • Bell pepper
  • Beet root
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Irish potatoes
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Chop the cabbage, green beans, spring onions, lettuce, beet root, cucumber and irish potatoes.
  2. Grate the carrots and slice the tomatoes and bell pepper.
  3. Mix everything together and serve with cream.

Salad

Description

This Salad recipe is delicious! Beet root and Irish potatoes are optional.

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Green beans
  • Spring onions
  • Lettuce
  • Bell pepper
  • Beet root
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Irish potatoes
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Chop the cabbage, green beans, spring onions, lettuce, beet root, cucumber and irish potatoes.
  2. Grate the carrots and slice the tomatoes and bell pepper.
  3. Mix everything together and serve with cream.

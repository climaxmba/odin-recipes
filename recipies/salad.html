Salad

Description

This Salad recipe is delicious! Beet root and Irish potatoes are optional.

Ingredients

  • Carbage
  • Carrots
  • Green beans
  • Spring onions
  • Lettuce
  • Bell pepper
  • Beet root
  • Irish potatoes
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Chop the carbage, green beans, spring onions, lettuce, beet root and irish potatoes.
  2. Grate the carrots and slice the bell pepper.
  3. Mix everything together and serve with cream.
